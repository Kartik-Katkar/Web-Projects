Diplocarpon earlianum is a fungus that causes leaf scorch, one of the most common leaf diseases of strawberry.

Solution for Disease / रोगाचा उपाय

If leaf diseases are a problem in the planting, fungicides will aid in control. However, the emphasis on control of leaf diseases should be placed on the use of resistant varieties.

The following junebearing varieties are reported to be resistant to both leaf spot and leaf scorch: Allstar, Canoga, Cardinal, Delite, Earliglow, Honeoye, Jewell, Lester, Midway and Redchief. The ever bearing varieties, Tribute and Tristar, are reported to be tolerant to leaf spot and leaf scorch. There are no varieties with reported resistance to leaf blight.
